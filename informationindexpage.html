What to do

Hot food

Enjoy hot food on the lively music festival trails. From spicy street tacos to sizzling kebabs, fish and chips and so much more. Don't forget to try our local, delicious and seasonal products.

Merchandaise

Did you know that the festival sells its own products to raise money for organizations in our city and Dunfermline. Visit our sales stand and collaborate with them.

Fairground rides

Look at this carousel, it looks fun. The laughter of festival goers mixes with the screams of joy of those who spin in the air on exciting rides, don't miss it.

Haunted house

You have been very scared...that mysterious facade hides a world of chilling emotions and disturbing surprises. The haunted house attracts those seeking a different type of adrenaline: a disturbing melody that causes chills.

What to see

Peter Bran

Originating from the heart of the city, The Echo Groove Collective is a fusion powerhouse. Combining jazz, funk and a touch of electronic elements, his music is a sonic journey that transcends genres. Known for their energetic live performances, they have become local favorites, turning every venue into a dance floor.

Rosie Roster

This hip-hop collective is a force to be reckoned with, tackling social issues with sharp lyrics and infectious beats. From local graffiti artists to dancers, their shows are a celebration of urban culture, breaking down barriers and giving a voice to the streets.

Sound Group

About Dunfermline

History

Dunfermline Musical Festival is a vibrant celebration of music that brings together local talent and renowned artists in a harmonious fusion. Nestled in the heart of Dunfermline, this annual event showcases a diverse range of musical genres, from classical and jazz to contemporary and folk. With a rich history, the Dunfermline Musical Festival has become a cultural highlight, attracting music enthusiasts from near and far. The festival provides a platform for emerging artists to share the stage with established performers, fostering a sense of community and musical discovery. From enchanting solo performances to electrifying ensemble acts, the festival caters to varied tastes and preferences. Attendees can immerse themselves in the melodic tapestry created by passionate musicians, creating lasting memories against the backdrop of Dunfermline's scenic charm. Beyond the music, the festival often features workshops, interactive sessions, and opportunities for aspiring musicians to learn and connect. The Dunfermline Musical Festival is not just an event; it's a harmonious celebration that resonates with the spirit of artistic expression, uniting people through the universal language of music.
